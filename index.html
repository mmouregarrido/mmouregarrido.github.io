About

I am an Assistant Professor in Telematics Engineering at Universidad Carlos III de Madrid. I obtained my PhD in October 2024.

My research focuses on network security, DNS privacy, encrypted traffic analysis, IoT security, and AI-driven threat detection.

Research Interests

  • Network security and encrypted traffic analysis
  • DNS privacy (DoH, DoT)
  • IoT fingerprinting and privacy protection
  • Federated learning for cybersecurity
  • AI-based network threat detection
